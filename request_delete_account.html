This page provides data deletion services for Jopar App users. If you wish to delete all personal data associated with your account, please follow the steps below. We value your privacy rights and are committed to processing your request promptly upon receipt.

Account data deletion is an irreversible operation. Once deleted, you will not be able to recover any data, including:

  • Personal profile information
  • App usage records
  • Settings and preferences
  • Other related data

How to Delete Your Data

Step 1: Send an email to our support address
Step 2: Clearly state in the email that you wish to delete your account data
Step 3: Provide your account-related information for identity verification
Step 4: We will process your request within 7 business days of receiving your email
